About Trivandrum

Trivandrum, also known as Thiruvananthapuram, is the capital city of Kerala. It is known for its beautiful beaches, cultural heritage, and historic temples.

Famous Places

  • Padmanabhaswamy Temple
  • Kovalam Beach
  • Napier Museum
  • Zoo

Special Features

The city is famous for its greenery, clean environment, and educational institutions. It is also a major IT hub with Technopark.
